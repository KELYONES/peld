Know what you drink

At PELD COOL WATERS, we provide pure, safe water through advanced reverse osmosis and careful packaging.

RO Water Treatment

Removes impurities by forcing water through a membrane, leaving it clean and refreshing.

Our system uses multiple steps to ensure purity:

Pre-filtration

Removes large particles like sand and rust.

Activated Carbon Filter

Eliminates chlorine, odors, and organic chemicals.

RO Membrane

Filters dissolved salts, bacteria, and viruses.

UV Sterilization

Uses UV light to kill remaining microorganisms.
